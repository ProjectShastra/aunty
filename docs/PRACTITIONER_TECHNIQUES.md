## The big convergent finding — validates our direction

**Nobody credible treats the 36-point Guna Milan total as a verdict.** Wilhelm: gates (Vedha/Rajju/Stree Deergha) + classical exceptions matter more than the total. DiCara: kutas measure *bonding strength* ("stickiness"), not happiness — "a high score means they stay stuck together, for better or worse." Braha: kuta points dilute marriage harmony into one-tenth of a multi-domain score. Rosen (2017): "not viable in modern times" as a standalone. KRS: refuses verdict-matching entirely. **Engine implication: our score is a ranking signal + conversation material, never a yes/no. Aunty's voice already says this; now the engine docs do too.**

**Second convergence: individual capacity first.** Wilhelm ("precompatibility"), Geppi (layer 1 of his 3D framework), DiCara (read each chart's marriage promise before the overlay), Rosen (7th-house "blind spots"). → Per-profile relationship indicators are a real layer, not just pair scores.
