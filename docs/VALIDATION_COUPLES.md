## Honest interpretation — exactly what the numbers say

- **Group means lean the right way:** lasting couples averaged **24.8/36**, divorced couples **20.3/36** (n=6 each). The best scores in the set (34, 32.5) belong to two of the most famously durable Hollywood partnerships, and the worst (12) to a documented divorce.
- **But as a binary predictor it is no better than chance.** Using the classical ≥18 "acceptable" line: 4/6 lasting couples pass (Clintons and Streisand-Brolin fail it) and 4/6 divorced couples *also* pass (Madonna-Penn scored 29.5). 50% accuracy. A 4.5-point mean gap with this much overlap on n=12 is a lean, not evidence.
- **This matches what the practitioner literature predicted** (docs/PRACTITIONER_TECHNIQUES.md): DiCara — kutas measure bonding strength, not happiness; Wilhelm — the total is meaningless without gates and exceptions; Cobain-Love scoring 26 *with a Nadi dosha and an AK/DK link* reads exactly like his "intensely bonded, not necessarily well" pattern.

## Caveats (all real, none excuses)

1. n=12 usable couples. Anecdote-scale, not statistics. No conclusions about predictive power are claimable either way.
2. Western celebrity couples — the system's native context is South Asian arranged-marriage vetting; marriage longevity in 20th-century Hollywood measures different social forces.
3. The ascendant formula is still the old unverified one (audit 3H, unfixed) — Manglik columns inherit that uncertainty. Guna scores depend only on the Moon (trustworthy: positions Swiss-validated).
4. Brad Pitt's time is A-rated with a known 6:21-vs-6:31 discrepancy; Madonna's and Penn's AA times have noted source quirks. Moon nakshatras are robust to ±10 min; ascendants are not.
5. Spot-check sanity: Diana's sidereal Moon → Dhanishta and JFK's → Purva Phalguni, both consistent with their published tropical Moons minus Lahiri ayanamsa.

## Implications

1. **Validates the product stance, not the scaremongering:** the score is a *ranking and narration* signal. Verdict language ("Not Recommended") on raw guna totals is indefensible against this data — Sprint 2 should soften verdict mapping and lean on Aunty's honest-but-warm framing.
2. **The discriminating power must come from the layers we haven't built yet:** Wilhelm's gates + classical exception rules (the Elizabeth-Philip Nadi dosha would be cancelled by a lord-exception check — worth testing), cross-chart degree synastry, 7th-house overlays, dasha context.
3. **Re-run this exact suite after each engine layer lands** — the harness and dataset are reusable; the interesting metric is whether group separation grows. Expanding to 30+ couples (and ideally Indian couples with published data) would make the numbers meaningful.
